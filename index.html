Bishkek
Bishkek, formerly Pishpek, or Bishkek, and Frunze, city and capital of Kyrgyzstan. It lies in the Chu River valley near the Kyrgyz Mountains at an elevation of 2,500–3,000 feet. Bishkek is situated along the Alaarcha and Alamedin rivers and intersects in the north with the Bolshoy Chuysky Canal.
